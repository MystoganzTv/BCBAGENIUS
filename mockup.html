Task E-01 · BCBA
Positive reinforcement is defined as:
A
Adding an aversive stimulus after a behavior to increase it
B
Removing a stimulus after a behavior to increase it
C
Adding a stimulus after a behavior that increases the future frequency of that behavior
✅
D
Removing a stimulus after a behavior that decreases its future frequency
✅ ¡Correcto!
Positive reinforcement involves the contingent delivery of a stimulus following a behavior that results in an increase in the future frequency of that behavior.
